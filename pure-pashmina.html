Pure 100% Pashmina

Our 100% pure Pashmina makes a perfect gift for someone you really care about. It is rich in its warmthness and luxury. Our pure pashmina is available in different sizes including shawl, stole, scarfts and blanket.

Our 100% pure Pashmina is available in 2 Ply and 4 Ply thickness. The term Ply represents the thickness of the yarn used in the weaving process. Therefore, a 4 Ply Pashmina will have more Pashmina than a 2 Ply Pashmina.
